A Neighbourhood Restaurant Like No Other

La Campagna is a wonderful neighbourhood restaurant where you can pop in at any time. Our menus cater for every occasion, whatever your appetite.

Pop in for a chat with friends over coffee or enjoy some of our lighter bites with a glass of bubbly. You are welcome to enjoy our relaxing and stylish interiors for a business lunch, a family celebration, or even just a drink from the bar and a snack.

The combination of modern and classic dishes creates an abundant and diverse menu of authentic Italian regional fare — from mixed antipasti plates brimming with cured ham, field mushrooms and goats cheese bruschetta, to mouth-watering risottos, pasta dishes and traditionally baked, inventive pizzas.
