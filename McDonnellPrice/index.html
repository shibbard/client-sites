Our Roofing Services

Covering every aspect of roofing from domestic repairs to large commercial projects across Gloucestershire and the South West.

Flat Roofing

Single ply, hot melt, liquid applied systems, bituminous felts and green roofing solutions with up to 20-year guarantees.

Learn more

Roof Tiling

Clay or concrete tiles in all profiles and formats, with dry fix solutions and expert ventilation specification.

Learn more

Slate Roofing

Natural slate to BS EN 12326, sourced from the UK or Spain, installed to BS5534:2003 by fully trained operatives.

Learn more

Lead Roofing Work

Rolled lead sheet flashings, valley work, bay and dormer windows, spires, cornices, parapets and box gutters.

Learn more

Building Refurbishment

Minor building works, bricklaying, loft insulation, dry stone walls and plastering for private and local authority clients.

Learn more

Historical Restoration

Award-winning restoration of listed and historic buildings, including Grade Listed projects in Cheltenham.
