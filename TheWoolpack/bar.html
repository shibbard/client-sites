Bar & Snug

Bar Kitchen

The heart of the inn

Step into the bar and you'll find an open fire crackling in the hearth, shelves lined with local ales and a chalkboard menu of honest bar snacks. The snug wraps around you like a well-worn armchair — this is the kind of place you come to stay a while.

We keep a rotating selection of real ales from local Cotswold breweries, alongside craft beers, fine ciders and a considered wine list. Bar kitchen snacks change with the seasons and are always made from scratch.
