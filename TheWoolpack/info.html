Work With Us

We are always excited to meet waiting staff, bartenders, cooks and kitchen staff. We are a community of passionate and enthusiastic individuals committed to creating a great working environment and to eating and drinking well.

If you have a desire to join a diverse community in a unique neighbourhood setting and enjoy looking after people, please do consider joining us.

Gift Vouchers

At The Woolpack we offer gift vouchers for every occasion. Treat your friends, colleagues and loved ones with a bespoke value voucher.

Perched on the side of the Slad Valley, we offer lunch and dinner six days a week throughout the year with spectacular views across the valley.

Vouchers are available from £25 and are valid for 12 months from purchase. Please call the pub to arrange yours.
