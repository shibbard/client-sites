A place of respite, fine ales, and seasonal fare, nestled in the golden stones of the Cotswold hills.

A warm welcome awaits at The Woolpack.

For over three centuries, these walls have echoed with the laughter of locals and weary travellers alike. We take pride in maintaining the character of our historic inn while offering a menu that celebrates the very best of British agriculture.

Whether you're joining us for a pint by the roaring fire in the Snug or a three-course feast in the Dining Room, we treat every guest like an old friend.
