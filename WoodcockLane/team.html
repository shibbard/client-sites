Practice Owner & Lead Dentist

Tara Conway

Restorative Prosthodontics Dentures GDC: 177315

Tara graduated from the University of Leeds in 2009 with a Merit and the Prosthodontics Prize. After vocational training and a Senior House Officer post in Oral and Maxillofacial Surgery at Frenchay and Southmead hospitals, she was awarded Membership to the Joint Dental Faculties at the Royal College of Surgeons in 2013.

In 2022 she completed a prestigious Masters of Clinical Dentistry in Fixed and Removable Prosthodontics at King's College London, qualifying with Distinction. She is particularly passionate about complex restorative dentistry, tooth wear and modern Scandinavian-designed dentures.

Tara took over ownership of Woodcock Lane Dental Care from David Lyons in March 2026.
