From routine check-ups to advanced cosmetic and restorative treatments — everything you need under one roof in Stonehouse.

Foundations of Oral Health

General Dentistry

We are pleased to offer routine dental care to you and your family to ensure healthy smiles. At every examination, after reviewing your medical status, we thoroughly check the health of your teeth and gums, as well as screening soft tissues for any problems including mouth cancer.

We use the very latest digital X-ray equipment, ensuring the lowest possible radiation dose. If treatment is advised, a written treatment plan and estimate will be issued. You will always have time to ask questions and discuss any concerns.

  • Comprehensive dental examinations
  • Digital X-rays with minimal radiation
  • Hygienist appointments from £98
  • White and silver fillings
  • Crowns, bridges & dentures
  • Sports mouthguards

Smile Enhancement

Cosmetic Dentistry

We believe your smile and dental health are of paramount importance. Using minimally invasive techniques, we achieve the best cosmetic results while preserving as much natural tooth structure as possible.

From simple polishing and composite bonding through to porcelain veneers and the latest metal-free crowns, we can improve the appearance of your smile and restore your confidence.

  • Tooth whitening (upper & lower from £430)
  • Composite bonding
  • Porcelain veneers
  • Tooth-coloured fillings
  • Emax metal-free crowns from £730
  • Free smile assessment

The Modern Alternative

Dental Implants

At Woodcock Lane Dental Care we offer dental implants as the modern alternative to false teeth and dentures. Our implant dentist, Guy Bailey, is highly qualified and immensely experienced — accepting both existing patients and referrals from other practices.

We use both the Straumann and Ankylos implant systems, both of which have a long track record of success producing excellent cosmetic results. Patients travel from Cheltenham, Gloucester, Bristol and beyond to benefit from our expertise.

  • Single tooth implants from £3,300
  • Full arch restorations
  • Implant-retained dentures
  • Straumann & Ankylos systems
  • Referrals welcome

Straighten Your Smile

Orthodontics

With over 35 years of experience and more than 3,000 successful cases, Dr David Lyons is one of the most experienced orthodontists in Gloucestershire. We offer a full range of treatments to gently straighten and align teeth.

NHS orthodontic assessments are available free of charge for children to determine if they qualify for NHS treatment. For those who don't qualify and for adults, a range of private options are available with flexible payment plans.

  • Fixed metal braces
  • Tooth-coloured braces
  • Invisible aligners
  • NHS treatment for qualifying children
  • Private from £2,400 to £6,330
  • Referrals from across South Gloucestershire

Family-Friendly Care

Children's Dentistry

Special attention has been given to the needs of our youngest patients, including a purpose-built playroom and incentives to encourage good dental habits. Children collect stars for having clean teeth and receive a prize when they have collected three!

We are able to provide NHS dental and orthodontic care to children under the age of 18 (or aged 18 and in full-time education) free of charge. We will advise both parents and children how a sensible diet and correct cleaning can prevent dental decay.

  • NHS care free for under-18s
  • Purpose-built play area
  • Star reward scheme
  • Diet and hygiene advice for families
  • Free NHS orthodontic assessments
