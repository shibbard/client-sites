Distressed Assets Group

Uniquely positioned to address the evolving challenges of today's commercial real estate market, leveraging over $500 million in family office capital to uncover high-potential opportunities nationwide.

What is distress?

In real estate, "distress" refers to properties or assets that are facing financial difficulties or physical challenges that lower their market value. These properties might be in foreclosure, owned by financially struggling entities, or require significant repairs. Distressed properties are often sold below market value, making them attractive for investors who can revitalize or reposition them for profit.

Market challenges & strategic investor-oriented solutions

The commercial real estate sector is navigating unprecedented headwinds — rising vacancy rates, escalating insurance costs, and a sharp decline in property values, with CRE properties trading at 50-60% discounts of their prior purchase values. Compounding these challenges is the inability to refinance maturing debt due to the tightening credit market. These dynamics are expected to persist for the next 3-5+ years, posing considerable risks but also creating substantial opportunities for strategic investors.

With over 100+ years' combined experience, our seasoned partners offer market-based, creative investor-oriented solutions — including rescue capital and joint venture equity options — to resolve and stabilize distressed assets. We specialize in private equity joint venture rescue capital, equity-profit sharing lending, and purchasing distressed notes or LLC interests tailored to support struggling projects.

Why partner with us?

Our Distress Division is agile and responsive, with the ability to make swift decisions and execute transactions efficiently. As a local family office-backed firm, we retain full discretion for all underwriting and deal-making decisions — not constrained by the slow-moving committee processes of traditional institutional investors.

Our "White Knight" approach allows us to assume any role necessary — joint venture partner, financial backer, or full asset purchaser. With deep expertise in CRE maturities, litigation, foreclosure, receiverships and more, we are prepared to step in and preserve value in the most challenging circumstances.
