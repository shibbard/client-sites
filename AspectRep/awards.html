Aspect wins CoStar's Q4 2021 Power Broker Quarterly Deals Award

February 2022 — Orange County, CA — CoStar Group, Inc., the leading provider of commercial real estate information, analytics and online marketplaces, announced the CoStar Power Broker Quarterly Deals winners for the fourth quarter of 2021. Gary Abraham, David Girty, David Stassel and Nick Sefarian of Aspect Real Estate Partners came out on top in the list of winners in Irvine. Winners are determined by the top deals executed every quarter, based on price and square footage.

The Aspect team was proud to represent The Goldfinch Restaurant in a 4,580 SF hard corner location at the newly constructed Eighteen Main project. Like the 908, its sister restaurant in Long Beach, The Goldfinch will be a place for power lunches and business dinners as well as a gathering spot for locals. With this transaction, Aspect adds to its standing as a trusted representative for experienced restaurant tenants.

For more information, visit costarpowerbrokers.com.
