What counts as one revision?

One round means you send us all your changes in a single list — wording, colours, photos, layout. We make them all. Additional rounds or new pages after that are quoted separately and are always reasonable.

What happens after year one?

We'll remind you before it's due — no auto-renewals, no nasty surprises. Just £99/year to keep your site live and hosted. That's under £10 a month, and you can cancel any time. Want to move elsewhere? We'll hand everything over, no questions asked.

Why so much cheaper than an agency?

Static sites have no database, no plugins, nothing to maintain or update. Combined with modern build tools that let us work fast without cutting corners, we can offer great results at a fraction of agency prices.
