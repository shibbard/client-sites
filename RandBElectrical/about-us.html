Our Story

About R & B Electrical

A family-founded business built on punctuality, professionalism, quality and integrity since 2007.

Who We Are

R & B Electrical Installations Ltd was founded in 2007 by Darren Redmond and Scott Bevan, who between them have over 30 years of electrical experience, working predominantly in the domestic and commercial sector.

Today, R & B Electrical Installations Ltd is an ever-expanding company with huge amounts of ambition. Through hard work and determination, the company is now among one of the leading electrical contractors in the Five Valleys and surrounding areas.

We pride ourselves on the standard of service from the first initial contact to the completion of your installation. Our beliefs in business are simple: to always demonstrate and maintain an attitude of punctuality, professionalism, quality and above all integrity.

With this philosophy we guarantee to carry out all of our work diligently and efficiently to ensure that you receive a high specification whilst getting the best value.
