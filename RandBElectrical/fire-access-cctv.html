Fire Detection, Access Control & CCTV

Protecting your property, staff and assets with professionally installed security and fire safety systems.

Fire Detection

Automatic fire detection is commonly used to protect property by ensuring the early attendance of the fire service. The need for a fire alarm system is normally determined by the authority responsible for enforcing fire safety legislation and/or a fire risk assessment.

In general, it is appropriate to install some form of fire alarm system in virtually all buildings. Whatever the size, complexity or application of your fire detection system, we have the expertise to help — installing, maintaining and monitoring your system to the relevant standards to comply with both Local Authority and insurers' requirements.

Access Control

Access Control Systems are designed and installed to provide protection against unwanted visitors and allow free movement for authorised users where there is frequent personnel traffic.

The correct Access Control solution provides protection to your employees, visitors, stock and property. In simple terms, Access Control Systems provide control of entry or exit through nominated doors via a control panel and electric locking facility. Solutions can be as simple or complex as you desire — always providing easy passage for permitted persons around the building.

CCTV

Vast improvements have been made in the CCTV industry, leading to improved picture quality and the introduction of digitally recorded evidence. High-speed, self-managing cameras are now used by our clients who need to protect their staff, buildings and profit, without interrupting day-to-day business.

Systems can be locally monitored by on-site security staff, or remotely monitored off-site by a specialised monitoring station. We provide full service and installation in domestic and commercial properties, along with offsite monitoring. Visual and audio connections are available.
