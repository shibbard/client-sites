Electrical Installation & Design

The core business of R & B Electrical Installations Ltd is the design and installation of electrical systems within the domestic and commercial sectors, undertaken to the highest standards and latest IEE wiring regulations.

Our service incorporates all aspects of design, installation, testing and inspection and covers all areas — from domestic fault finding to complete re-wiring of private dwellings and retail units.

Our highly specialised team can design your project from the initial planning stage through to final completion, whether it's a minor alteration or a complete re-wire or new installation. Our team will deliver with the highest standards of service.

Another key aspect of our work is carrying out responsive repairs and electrical safety checks for the private and letting agency sector, as well as PAT testing. A periodic inspection report is essential for all landlords and home owners — every electrical installation will deteriorate with age and use, so it's vital that each installation is serviceable and above all safe.
