Generate Cheap, Green Electricity

In a time when we're not just concerned with our carbon footprint and climate change, but rising energy bills and the amount of money energy providers are making, more emphasis is being put on renewable energy sources — Solar (PV) panels being one of them.

As an affordable alternative, Solar PV is something many home owners and businesses are now investing in to reap rewards for the future. Renewable energy is no less reliable than energy generated from more traditional sources, and using it does not mean you have to change your lifestyle or appliances.

R & B Electrical provides a complete Solar PV service incorporating all aspects of design, installation, testing and inspection — ensuring your system performs safely and efficiently from day one.

Benefits

Why Go Solar?

Solar PV makes economic and environmental sense for homes and businesses across the UK.

Reduce Your Energy Bills

Generate your own electricity and significantly reduce your monthly energy costs, with the potential to sell surplus energy back to the grid.

Lower Carbon Footprint

Solar energy is clean and renewable. Installing panels helps reduce your home or business's contribution to carbon emissions and climate change.

Long-Term Investment

Solar PV systems have a lifespan of 25+ years. Once installed, they provide decades of clean energy and an excellent return on investment.
