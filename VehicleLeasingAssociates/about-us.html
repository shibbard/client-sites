Welcome to Vehicle Leasing Associates (UK)

Vehicle Leasing Associates (UK) brings together a group of associates whose extensive combined purchasing power means we can secure genuinely cost-effective leasing — tailored to individuals or business. Included in our experience is 17 years of being associated with one of Europe's leading finance institutions.

From tradespeople — builders, plumbers and electricians — to families and businesses of every size, we help our clients into the right new car or van, on the right terms, with none of the guesswork. Our specialist knowledge and panel of funders set us apart from typical broker operations.

Our Expertise

Specialist Knowledge & Buying Power

Light Commercial Vehicles

We cater to all sizes of vans and pickup trucks, understanding the unique needs of tradespeople who rely on these vehicles for their day-to-day operations. Our long-standing experience in this sector allows us to offer tailored solutions that meet the specific demands of our clients.

Small / Medium Cars

Many individuals driving used cars spend a significant amount on maintenance and MOTs, believing a new car is beyond their budget. Leasing with us changes that. With fixed monthly payments ranging from £100 to £500, driving a new car becomes not only affordable but economical — a more enjoyable and reliable driving experience.
