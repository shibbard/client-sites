The Benefits of Switching to Electric

Are Electric Cars Worth It?

There are lots of reasons why people are ditching the pump for a plug. Electric cars are cheaper to lease than a gas guzzler when you take into account no petrol or diesel cost.

With no tailpipe emissions they're great for the environment too. And even diehard petrolheads can't deny that electric cars are relaxing and easy to drive.

  • Better for the environment
  • Very low running cost
  • Less maintenance
  • Easy & convenient to drive
Behind the Wheel

What's It Like to Drive an Electric Car?

It's easy to focus on the cost and environmental benefits of electric cars, but they're also a lot of fun to drive. Unlike a conventional engine, electric motors produce their maximum torque instantly from standstill.

Take a Look at the Following

Why Drivers Are Making the Switch

Instant Acceleration

Improved acceleration and unrivalled zippiness around town.

Engaging Handling

A low centre of gravity from floor-mounted batteries makes them engaging to drive.

Smooth & Quiet

Less noise and fewer vibrations than combustion engines — smooth and effortless.

Fewer Gears

Fewer or no gears to worry about makes electric cars less tiring and easier to drive.

Swap Fuel for a Lease

Consider replacing the petrol cost of your current vehicle for a lease payment on a brand-new electric model.

Zero Emissions

No tailpipe emissions means cleaner air and a positive statement on the environment.
